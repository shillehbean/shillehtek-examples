# ESP32-DevKitC-VE

The ESP32-DevKitC-VE is a development board based on the ESP32-WROVER with 8 MB flash and 8 MB PSRAM pre-soldered. It’s suitable for Wi‑Fi/Bluetooth projects and memory‑intensive tasks such as camera streaming, buffering large datasets, or running lightweight ML inference. The examples below show how to confirm PSRAM, join Wi‑Fi, and scan the I2C bus under MicroPython.
